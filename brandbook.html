Ganesha Brandbook

1. Brand Philosophy

Mission

"To nourish body and soul with divine plant-based treats that honor the planet and uplift the spirit."

Brand Slogan

"Immerse yourself in the colors of divine bliss."

Our Values (The Mantra)

  • Ahimsa (Non-violence): 100% Vegan. We believe true pleasure should not cause harm to living beings.
  • Sattva (Purity): Only natural ingredients. No chemicals, hormones, or artificial additives. Pure energy for a pure mind.
  • Ananda (Bliss): Food is a celebration. Our flavors are vibrant, unexpected, and bring childlike joy with every scoop.

Tone of Voice

"Enlightened Playfulness"

We communicate like a wise but fun friend. We use spiritual references ("karma", "zen", "vibes") lightly and with a smile, never preachy.

  • ✅ "Send your taste buds an invite to nirvana."
  • ❌ "We manufacture a high-quality lactose-free product."

1.1 Target Audience

Profile: "The Conscious Hedonist"

Demographics

  • Age: 20–40 (Gen Z & Millennials).
  • Location: Major cities, urbanites.
  • Income: Middle+, willing to pay for quality and ethics.

Psychographics

  • Cares about health but won't sacrifice taste.
  • Interested in yoga, meditation, self-growth, or "slow living".
  • Eco-activist at heart (recycles, chooses cruelty-free).
  • Loves travel and exotic flavors (India, Bali, Thailand).

2. Logo

The "Ganesha G" Icon. A warm, approachable 'G' icon featuring a playful, young Ganesha. The design is inviting and joyous, perfectly capturing the "Enlightened Playfulness" of the brand.

3. Color Palette

Our "Chakra Feast" palette balances energy with serenity. It ignores standard digital neon in favor of rich, spice-market hues that feel organic yet luxurious. These colors are chosen to stimulate appetite while grounding the spirit.

Saffron Spice #E76F51 Primary. Stimulates appetite and represents creative energy (Sacral Chakra). Warm, inviting, not aggressive.
Midnight Mantra #264653 Text/Base. Deep charcoal-green. Represents deep wisdom and grounding. Softer than black, more premium than grey.
Vedic Teal #2A9D8F Secondary. Balances the heat of saffron. Represents nature, veganism, and tranquility without being cliché "leaf green".
Turmeric Gold #E9C46A Accent. Illuminating and rich. Adds a touch of divinity and premium quality (Solar Plexus).
Papaya Sunset #F4A261 Supporting. A softer bridge between Saffron and Gold. Represents playfulness and fruitfulness.
Coconut Milk #F9F7F2 Background. Warm off-white. Pure, edible, and comforting. Avoids the sterility of clinical white.

Psychological Harmony

This palette uses a Double-Split Complementary scheme logic but properly muted for a premium feel.

  • Warm Tones (Saffron, Gold, Papaya): Trigger hunger, warmth, and joy (Hedonism).
  • Cool Tones (Teal, Midnight): Provoke trust, calm, and signify "natural/organic" (Consciousness).

4. Typography

Typography Concept: The Dance of Tradition & Modernity

We pair Ramaraja, a highly readable serif with classic Indian modulation, with Outfit, a clean geometric sans-serif that speaks to modern vegan purity. This combination balances the brand's soulful heritage (Ganesha) with clear, accessible communication.

Headings: Ramaraja

Used for H1, H2, and logos. A modulated serif that provides the distinct feel of Indian calligraphy but with stronger, more uniform strokes for excellent readability.

Body Text: Outfit

Geometric sans-serif, modern, clean, and highly legible. It balances the ornate headings with simplicity.

Outfit Bold (700) — Used for Buttons & Accents

Outfit Regular (400) — The standard weight for all body text, conveying information clearly without distraction. Perfect for ingredient lists and storytelling.

Outfit Light (300) — utilized for subtle captions, disclaimers, or elegant sub-text.
